Data originates from PostGIS (POI table with 100k+ rows, AOI polygons, land-use parcels). The postgis skill fetches raw rows filtered by WKT geometry and category. Rows feed into area insight templates (H3 grid, ring distribution, category histogram) that produce aggregated buckets. The spatial encoder (Python) consumes these buckets to produce semantic feature tags. The spatial vector (FAISS) consumes pre-computed embeddings. All streams converge in an EvidenceViewFactory typed view.
